Controls

move tank: W, A, S, D

rotate turret horiontally: Q and E

change turret elevation: mouse scroll wheel

fire: SPACE

mark range on projectile plot: left mouse button (while a range is marked, turret rotation is slowed for longer ranges)

Try to hit the planes. The altitudes of the planes are diplayed next to them.
